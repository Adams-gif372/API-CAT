About Us

We are a patient centered hospital dedicated to delivering high quality, compassionate healthcare through advanced medical technology and experienced professionals.

We are located in South B along Mchumbi road and opposite Cleanshelf Supermarket.

Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable and high quality healthcare services while upholding dignity, compassion and respect for every patient.

Our Services

Emergency Care

Our emergency department provides 24/7 rapid response and critical care for accidents, injuries, and life-threatening conditions.

Laboratory Services

Equipped with lab technology and our team offers timely results and effective treatment planning.

Inpatient and surgical services

We offer comprehensive inpatient care and advanced surgical procedures supported by modern operating theaters and skilled specialists.
